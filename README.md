## About

QRTifact is a production-ready backend platform that connects museum visitors with exhibit content through QR codes. Museums register their exhibits with multilingual descriptions, images, and audio guides. Visitors scan a QR code and instantly access rich content in their preferred language.

---

## Features

- **Scan QR codes** — visit any exhibit and scan its QR code to instantly open the exhibit page
- **Choose your language** — read and listen to exhibit content in Uzbek, Russian, or English
- **Audio guides** — listen to a narrated audio guide for each exhibit in your language
- **Explore museums** — browse exhibits by museum, hall, or category
- **Save favorites** — bookmark exhibits you want to revisit later
- **Google sign-in** — log in quickly with your Google account
- **Buy scan credits** — purchase credit packages to unlock premium exhibits
- **Pay with local methods** — supports Payme and Click (Uzbek payment providers)
